Gridlee was an arcade game that was never released. It was developed by Videa, inc. in 1982. Wait... Did I just say it emulates an arcade cabinet? Yes... But how? MAME4iOS, that's how. MAME4iOS is an arcade emulator for iOS. To get the full features to show up, just insert another ROM into the ROMs folder, inside the Documents folder. You can do so with iFunbox.
