GBA4iOS 1.6 is the predecessor to GBA4iOS 2.x.x. It should only be used if you are still on iOS 6. If you are on iOS 7, try GBA4iOS 2.x.x.

 

GBA4iOS is based on ZodTTD's gpSPhone, a GameBoy Advance emulator. It can play most GBA games. If you are on iOS 6 and lived during the GBA's popularity peak, and want to recreate your childhood memories, this is the app for you. Or even if you weren't around for the GBA, or it wasn't of interest to you back then, this is a way to try it out!

 

 Games can be either put into the documents folder though iTunes File Sharing, or added by using the Open In function in Safari or other apps after downloading a ROM.
